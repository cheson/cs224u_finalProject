CONTENTS:

* WordSim353: http://www.cs.technion.ac.il/~gabr/resources/data/wordsim353/wordsim353.zip

* imdb-wordword.csv: word x word distributional vectors derived from http://ai.stanford.edu/~amaas/data/sentiment/index.html

* imdb-worddoc.csv: word x document distributional vectors derived from http://ai.stanford.edu/~amaas/data/sentiment/index.html

* question-data: the word analogies tables distributed with the glove code from http://nlp.stanford.edu/projects/glove/

* longer_shuffled_synset_relations.tsv: WordNet derived entailment data from Bowman, Potts, and Manning's experiments  

* imdb-posneg-lexicon.pickle: Bing Liu's sentiment lexicon restricted to the vocabulary of imdb-wordword.csv (same as imdb-worddoc.csv), with a random train/test split for the positive and negative categories
